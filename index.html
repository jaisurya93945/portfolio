My Projects

USB-Analyzer is an advanced cyber security tool I developed to enhance the safety and integrity of digital systems by monitoring and analyzing USB devices. It employs sophisticated algorithms to detect and prevent threats from malicious USBs, ensuring robust protection against unauthorized data access and cyber attacks. This tool continuously scans connected USB devices, scrutinizing their behavior and content in real-time to identify and neutralize potential risks. By providing instant alerts and taking preventive actions, USB-Analyzer helps safeguard sensitive information and mitigate cyber threats. Its user-friendly interface offers clear reports on USB activity and security incidents, making it an essential component in modern cyber security strategies for both individuals and organizations.

Malware Generator is a project I've developed to simulate various types of malware for research and testing purposes in the field of cyber security. This tool allows security professionals to generate custom malware samples, which can then be used to test and strengthen defenses against real-world threats. By providing a controlled environment for the creation and analysis of malware, Malware Generator helps identify vulnerabilities in systems and improve detection and mitigation strategies. The tool's user-friendly interface makes it accessible for researchers and IT professionals, enabling them to understand the behavior and impact of different malware types without the risk of uncontrolled spread. This project is essential for advancing cyber security research and enhancing the robustness of digital defenses.
